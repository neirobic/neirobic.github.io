COVID-SAFE EVENTS

If you are the host of a COVID-safe event and would like it to be added to our list, please get in touch!

To be able to advertise your event as COVID-safe, the following precautions need to be met:

  • Masks required - KN95 or higher
  • Filtration which meets the (smth) standard

ABOUT

Our group grew out of a desire to dedicate more energy to activism and advocacy around COVID awareness and safety. Initially part of Mask4Mask-MTL, a Discord server which aims to connect the queer COVID-aware community of Montreal.
